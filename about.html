About Shane Hingtgen

Biography of Shane Hingtgen

Shane Anthony Hingtgen currently works as a Distribution Field Designer at an engineering firm. He is currently going to school at Bellevue University to become a Web Developer and will graduate in the spring of 2024. Previously he was working as a Wind Technician and would travel around troubleshooting and maintaining wind turbines. From an early age he has never had a fear of heights, according to his parents, at age six he snuck up the ladder behind his father who was installing a satellite dish at their house.

Shane grew up camping every weekend with his family and they would go on vacations to national parks over summer break while in high school, it was a fantastic experience. One day he hopes to bring his wife and kids on those same vacations and even more of them around the country. His hobbies are fishing, hunting, hiking and gaming with his brothers. Shane is excited for the future, and cannot wait to share the enjoyment of his hobbies with his kids when they get old. Of course Shane and his Wife, Ally only have a one year old currently, but plan on having more kids upon graduation in the Spring of 2024. Shane is also planning on volunteering in wildlife conservation groups after graduation because he loves the outdoors and its incredible whenever people get together to open more public wildlife places for everyone to enjoy. Some of Shane's favorite places to visit have been North East Iowa, the Black Hills, Yellowstone National Park and the Grand Tetons National Park.
